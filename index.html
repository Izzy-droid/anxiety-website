More on Anxiety healing

How to learn more about your anxiety and it's effects on daily life. Anxiety is one of the most common mood disorders in America. Characterized by excessive worry, fear, or stress, it can lead to a range of emotional and physical symptoms.


Generalized Anxiety Disorder

It's easy for anxiety to take over your life, depending on what it manifests as. In some this is intrusive thoughts invading your mind daily, others it's the fear of physical symptoms or loss of control during panic attacks.


Panic Disorder

Panic attacks can be a common occurrence in people with anxiety, it is sudden moment of intense fear and include physical symptoms like:

  • Chest pain
  • Headache
  • Shortness of breath.

Depending on the person, the attack can last for hours or even days.
